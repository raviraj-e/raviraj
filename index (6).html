HOW DOES IT WORK?

They function similarly to mutual funds, where
you purchase shares that represent ownership in a
portfolio of income-generating properties

Simple &
Rewarding

Investing in REITs has a lot of perks and benefits that makes it unique and a must have in your portfolio

More About REITs

Low Investment Threshold

REITs allow you to invest in a diversified
of properties, mitigating risks
associated with a single property



Stable and Consistent Returns

REITs generate income through long-term
leases, providing a reliable source of income
that can help weather market fluctuations



Diversification

Compared to directly buying real estate,
REITs offer a lower entry point, making them
accessible to a broader range of investors



Hedge Against Inflation

Rental income typically increases with
inflation, offering a hedge against rising
prices

Mindspace Business Parks REIT

An India-based real estate

Embassy Office Parks REIT
